## Projects

### [Xora](https://github.com/Eben-Siyabalapitiya) — WiFi CSI Motion Detection
Four ESP32 boards passively capture WiFi Channel State Information to detect human presence and movement — no camera, no radar, no wearable. Feeds into a live Flask/WebSocket dashboard with calibration and real-time data collection.
`ESP32` `Python` `Flask` `WebSocket` `WiFi CSI`

---

### Auto-Targeting Turret
Computer vision turret that locks onto red targets in real time. Calculates position offset and distance from the camera feed, then sends aim commands over serial to an ESP32.
`Python` `OpenCV` `YOLO` `ESP32` `Serial`

---

### Plant Disease Detector
Trained on 70,000+ images across 38 disease classes. Takes a photo of a plant leaf and identifies the disease with 93.5% accuracy. Converted to TFLite for edge deployment.
`TensorFlow` `TFLiteConverter` `Computer Vision`

---

### Sign Language Translator (MNDX)
Real-time ASL recognition using MediaPipe hand landmarks + a custom Keras model trained on 14 signs locally. Built as part of MNDX, an initiative to deliver open-source assistive devices to Canadians with disabilities.
`MediaPipe` `TensorFlow/Keras` `pyttsx3` `Streamlit`

---

### LUMA — ESP32 Desktop Companion
Minimal desk companion with a TFT display and capacitive touch. Ongoing hardware/software build — currently solving display driver configs on the ST7735S.
`ESP32` `TFT_eSPI` `C++`
